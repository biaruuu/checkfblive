The Check Live UID Facebook tool allows you to verify the status of Facebook User IDs (UIDs) in bulk. Simply enter UIDs one per line and click Check to see which accounts are live, dead, or returned an error.

This tool supports up to 50 UIDs per batch and provides multiple view modes to suit your workflow. Results are clearly categorized so you can easily copy or export each group.

Use the view mode buttons to toggle between UID-only, full detail, separated, or compact views of your results.

What is a Facebook UID?
A Facebook UID (User ID) is a unique numeric identifier assigned to every Facebook account. It typically starts with "100" and is 15–16 digits long.
How many UIDs can I check at once?
You can check up to 50 UIDs per batch. Enter one UID per line in the text area.
What do the status categories mean?
Live = the account exists and is active. Dead = the account has been deactivated or deleted. Error = the check could not be completed for that UID.
What are the different view modes?
UID — shows only the UID numbers. Full — shows UID with status. Separated — groups by status with labels. Compact — condensed single-line format.
Why do some UIDs show as Error?
Errors can happen due to network issues, rate limiting, or invalid UID formats. Try re-checking those UIDs after a short wait.
